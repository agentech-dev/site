# surfaced

surfaced helps teams monitor how their brand and product appears in LLM-generated answers.

## Overview

LLMs are increasingly how people discover and evaluate products. surfaced tracks what models say about you, your competitors, and your category - so you can understand and improve your AI-search presence.

## Features

- Monitor brand and product mentions across major LLMs
- Track competitor positioning in AI-generated answers
- Analyze how LLM responses about your brand change over time
- CLI-first interface designed for agent workflows

## Getting started

surfaced is operated via its CLI. See the [documentation](/surfaced/docs) for setup and usage.
